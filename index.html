FAQ

Reasonable questions.

Does it work without a PMS?

Yes. Mintmango can be the source of truth or sync with your PMS. Migration is bidirectional.

What if there’s no wifi on the floors?

The mobile app runs offline and syncs when it reconnects. Housekeeping never loses their list.

How long does implementation take?

40 rooms: one shift. 400 rooms: 3–5 days with an assigned CSM.

Is our data ours?

Always. Export everything to CSV or Parquet in one click. Zero lock-in.
